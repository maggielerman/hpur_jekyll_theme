Johnson Nathan Strohe

Branding

Creative Direction & Design

Johnson Nathan Strohe is an award winning architectural firm that has been shaping the urban landscape of Colorado for over 20 years. The firm came to me with the challenge of rebranding the company from JG Johnson Architects to Johnson Nathan Strohe - marking a shift in internal leadership, and also to better reflect their progressive vision and increasingly diverse client base. I worked closely with Director of Brand Strategy, Roberta Reis, to achieve a new identity that retains a sense of equity and traditional values, but also reflects their progressive and modern vision. The new visual identity offers JNS the competitive edge they need to compete in an increasingly saturated market, and also thrive in the booming economy that is Denver, Colorado - one of the most rapidly developing cities in the United States.

Bear Creek Distillery

Branding & Package Design

Creative Direction & Design

Armed with nothing but honest ingredients and pure passion for small batch spirits, Bear Creek Distillery is quickly gearing up to make a big splash in the rapidly growing Colorado craft spirit scene. The Bear Creek identity is clean and modern in style - much like the way a fine whiskey takes on its unique character and flavor profile after being distilled, the shape of the bear icon has itself been distilled down to only its essential features and lines. Both the product and the brand capture the spirit of the West and what it means to be uniquely from Colorado.

Next: Menagerie

Menagerie

Branding

Creative Direction & Design

An artful collection of strange and diverse objects - the definition of Menagerie. Founder, Andy Raether, is a world class climber and entrepreneur. He started Menagerie out of a deep appreciation for the natural world and the simple desire to climb with better holds. Fusing both artful form and unyielding function, Raether's unique climbing holds are inspired by nature and refined through meticulous research and design. The brand needed to embody this delicate balance between aesthetics and engineering, while at the same time appeal to climbers of many different skill levels and backgrounds. Since launching in 2013, Menagerie has seen incredible growth and has become a leading brand in the world of indoor climbing. Their bold shapes are shipped all over the world and enjoyed by climbers who share a similar passion for artful expression through this demanding sport. Menagerie encourages people to climb like no one is watching!

Next: Roniit

Roniit

Branding, Web Design, Motion

Creative Direction & Design

Roniit is quickly making a name for herself in the musical realm of Dark Pop. She has an international fan base who share an appreciation for the darker things in life. Roniit has relied on me on a consistent basis for the past several years to fulfill her branding and visual needs as an alternative musical artist. I have completed a multitude of different projects for Roniit, the most prominent being the design of her personal website and most recent music video for the song 'Runaway'. The website needed to be versatile, lightweight, and reflective of her unique style of music. The landing page features timed full page announcements for things such as new music releases or press. A fully interactive photo gallery and tour schedule was also integrated. Working with developer, Keith Driessen, we built an immersive web experience for her fans, along with ensuring seamless mobile integration. In addition to aiding in the digital aspects of her brand, I have also helped in developing her photographic representation. I recently directed and conceptualized the music video for her latest single, 'Runaway'. With months of planning, a limited budget, and only one day allotted for filming, the final result is a beautifully dark expression that has fueled her career and elevated her perception as an emerging force to be reckoned with.

Next: Tenin

Tenin

Branding

Art Direction & Design

Tenin offers custom timepieces that are tailored to the modern gentleman. Each Tenin watch is built by hand by founder and watchmaker, Paul Krucke, and features fully mechanical functionality. The original logo was concepted by Krucke himself. The mark not only mimicks a key mechanical part in the watch, but is also a typographic expression - each letter in the logo (T-E-N-I-N) can be found subtly whithin this simple mark. He approached me to refine what he had laid the foundation for and to build out a complete visual identity system for the brand. The final product is a brand that appeals directly to patrons who have an undeniable appreciation for bespoke timepieces and the mechanics under the surface that make them tick.

Next: Re Source

Re Source

Branding & Exhibition Design

Design

My senior thesis at the University of Colorado revolved around the issue of waterborne illness in Africa, and how design can affect and inspire charity among the wealthy. Over a four month period, I sought to discover how I could utilize aspects of branding and print design to create an informative campaign targeted towards the wealthiest percentage of Americans, in order to inspire donations that can help alleviate water-borne illness in Africa. I partnered with Project Education South Sudan in order to gain insight to the situation in South Sudan, which provided the content I needed to create an informative printed and hand-stitched booklet. The end result was an interactive print-based installation at Redline Gallery in Denver that aimed to challenge people's perception of wealth and what it means for cultures other than our own.

Flyleaf

Motion

Art Direction & Design

When the opportunity arose to create a music video for the popular band Flyleaf, it sounded like an exciting endeavor that would surely push my creative skills and offer new and interesting challenges.
